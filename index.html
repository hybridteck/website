LOCATION The Proposed CIES-EETC Energy LTD estate project is a unique real estate development offering various housing types. It would be located in Benin Metropolis Edo State. The Proposed CIES-EETC Estate project would be one of the most ambitious housing projects to be undertaken in the country in recent times. LAYOUT The proposed Project is made up of diverse housing types ranging from detached bungalows to mini-duplexes. In addition, the overall site layout incorporates important design factors such as cross ventilation, safety, security and easy pedestrian and vehicular access. STYLE CIES-EETC projects combines city living with urban serenity. The end result is an estate equipped with top level amenities(including proper parking space, 24 hour security, central sewage and a water treatment plant) all sited in an exception & environment. TITLE INFORMATION The unexpired term of the Certificate of Occupancy covering the land will be transferred to the subscribers via a Deed of Assignment thus giving each buyer a full ownership of the leasehold on the property purchased. ROJECT TIME-LINE physical construction of the first phase is scheduled to commence on Government Approval with livery of houses to the first set of buyers from the formal sector or from the informal sector expected by Six Months into construction. It is anticipated at subscribers will become residents within 18 months of commitment to this project.
